Best Pace and Corral Calculator for New York Road Runners' Races

Enter your best race time and select the distance.







How is my best pace calculated?

It is calculated by taking the time from your best race (distance must be 5K and above), converting it to an equivalent time for a 10K, and calculating the per mile pace of the 10K.

Note: This webpage is not affiliated with NYRR and was made as a form of project-based learning. Corrals and best pace may not be identical to your actual corral and pace.
