Best Pace and Corral Calculator for New York Road Runners Races

Enter your best race time and select the distance.






How is my best pace calculated?

It is calculated by taking the time from your best race (over 5 K), converting it to an equivalent time for a 10K, and taking the per mile pace of the 10 K.

What is my best pace used for?

It's used to determine the corral you're in!
